# 🏗️ Equation Architect
A reverse-math logic "Trail" game where learners construct equations left-to-right to hit a specific target number.

## Highlights
- **Guaranteed Solvability**: Every puzzle generated is validated to have a linear solution.
- **Live Preview**: Real-time calculation box shows current progress as blocks are placed.
- **Left-to-Right Logic**: Brackets are removed to simplify the experience for children, focusing on linear chain-math.
- **Detailed Summary**: Final report shows level-by-level performance with correct answers for missed questions.
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: Three distinct age-appropriate tiers.
- **Accessible & Offline**: High contrast mode, multiple themes, and zero network dependency.

## Getting Started
1. Open the file in any modern browser.
2. Select your difficulty (Apprentice is default) and theme.
3. Click **START TRAIL** to begin.

## How to Play
- Look at the **Target Result**.
- Tap numbers and operators to build your "Blueprint".
- You must use **every number block** once.
- Check the **Result Box** on the right to see if you hit the target.
- Use **Skip** if you get stuck on a difficult level.

## Difficulty Tuning
| Level | Age | Operations | Target Range |
|---|---|---|---|
| Apprentice | 6-8 | +, - | 10-25 |
| Builder | 9-10 | +, -, * | 20-60 |
| Master | 11-12 | +, -, *, / | 30-100 |
